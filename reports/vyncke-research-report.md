## Executive Summary

Vyncke is a fourth-generation Belgian family business founded in 1912, specializing in designing and building green and clean energy plants that convert biomass and industrial waste into thermal energy and electrical power. The company has grown into a global leader with over 430 employees across 10 locations worldwide and generates approximately 120 million euros in annual revenue.

---

## Company Overview

### Core Business
- **Industry:** Renewable Energy Equipment Manufacturing / Clean Energy Technology
- **Specialization:** Biomass boilers and waste-to-energy plants
- **Solutions:** Thermal energy (1-100 MWth) and electrical power (0.5-20 MWe)
- **Market Segments:** Food & agriculture, wood processing, recovered fuels

### Global Presence
- **Headquarters:** Harelbeke, Belgium (West Flanders)
- **International Locations:** Brazil, Ivory Coast, Spain, Germany, Czech Republic, India, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, China
- **Employee Count:** 370-430+ employees (sources vary)
- **Revenue:** Approximately 120 million euros annually
- **References:** Over 4,400 installations worldwide
- **Geographic Distribution:** Belgium (3%), Europe (45%), Outside EU (52%)

### Ownership & Leadership Structure
- Fourth-generation family business
- Co-CEOs: Peter Vyncke and Dieter Vyncke (since 2002)
- Supervisory Board in place for governance
