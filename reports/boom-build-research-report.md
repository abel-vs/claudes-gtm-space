### What They Do
Boom builds AI-driven underwriting software for surety bond companies, helping them analyze contractor work-in-progress (WIP) reports with unprecedented speed and accuracy. The platform automates data extraction, standardizes reporting, and identifies risk patterns in contract surety underwriting for the construction industry.

### Mission/Vision
"Building the AI-Interface for Surety" - Boom helps sureties build, implement, and maintain software and data systems to modernize the surety underwriting process.

## Product & Technology

### Core Products

**1. WIP Report Analysis Platform**
- Analyzes contractor work-in-progress reports with AI-driven automation
- Core product for surety underwriting decisions

**2. Automated Data Extraction**
- Extracts financial and project data from contractor WIP reports
- Eliminates manual spreadsheet work

**3. Standardized Reporting**
- Converts varied contractor reporting formats into standardized outputs
- Enables apples-to-apples comparisons across contractors

**4. Overbillings/Underbillings Analysis**
- Tracks underbillings (work done but not yet billed) - a key risk indicator for underwriters
- Identifies overbillings that may indicate future cash flow issues

**5. Project Trend Reports**
- Monitors project performance over time
- Identifies profit fade or gain patterns

**6. Gain/Fade Tracking**
- Analyzes changes in estimated project profitability vs. actuals
- Critical for assessing contractor estimating accuracy and project risk

**7. Detailed Underwriting Reports**
- Provides extra attention for exceptionally large or challenging projects
- Helps underwriters find data that is often hard to locate manually

### Key Features
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Machine learning algorithms to identify patterns and red flags
- **80% Efficiency Improvement**: Versus manual processes (per customer feedback)
- **Speed**: Significantly faster than traditional manual WIP review
- **Risk Detection**: Automatically flags concerning trends (profit fade, underbillings, cash flow issues)

### Technology Stack

### Integrations

## Market Trends & Context

### Surety Market Growth
- **U.S. Market Size**: $9.3B in premiums (2023), up from $8.6B (2022) - 8% growth
- **Global Market**: $19.62B (2024), projected to reach $27B by 2030
- **Contract Surety Share**: 58% of total surety market (largest segment)
- **Infrastructure Driver**: $1.2 trillion U.S. infrastructure bill creating increased demand for surety bonds

### Technology Adoption Trends
- **Digital Transformation**: Surety industry historically slow to adopt technology, creating opportunity for disruptors
- **AI/ML Adoption**: Industry increasingly embracing AI for risk assessment, fraud detection, and underwriting accuracy
- **Blockchain**: Some carriers exploring blockchain for transparency and fraud reduction
- **Speed Expectations**: Customers expect instant or same-day bond issuance vs. weeks-long traditional process
- **Data Integration**: Need for real-time data from construction management platforms (Procore, Autodesk)

### Industry Challenges Boom Addresses
1. **Manual WIP Review**: Underwriters spend hours/days reviewing contractor WIP reports in spreadsheets
2. **Inconsistent Reporting**: Contractors provide WIP data in different formats, making comparison difficult
3. **Red Flag Detection**: Easy to miss subtle indicators of contractor financial distress (profit fade, underbillings)
4. **Slow Underwriting**: Traditional underwriting takes days/weeks, creating bottlenecks
5. **Underwriter Shortage**: Experienced surety underwriters are scarce, increasing value of automation
